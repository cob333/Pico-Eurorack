How upload .uf2 to my Pico/PicoFX?
  1. DO NOT use your eurorack power;
  2. Hold the "BOOT" button while plugging the USB-C cable into your computer;
  3. After that, release the "BOOT" button;
  4. You will see a folder "RP2350" in your device;
  5. Drag the generated ".uf2" into this folder, wait 'till it's done;
  6. Get ready with your new Pico/PicoFX~

How do I change apps while using Pico/PicoFX?
  1. Hold the button while powering on your system.
  2. Click the button to move to the next app. The LED color matches the slot color.
  3. Hold the button to enter the selected app. On a normal boot, Pico/PicoFX automatically opens the last app you used.
